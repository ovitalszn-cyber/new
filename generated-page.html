Common Questions

How fast are the odds updated?
We poll major books every 1-3 seconds. For our Pro and Scale plans, we utilize push-based updates where available to ensure near real-time latency.
Do you offer player prop mapping?
Yes. This is our core value proposition. We map "Points + Rebounds" across 15+ books to a single ID, handling naming discrepancies (e.g., "Giannis Antetokounmpo" vs "G. Antetokounmpo") automatically.
Can I access historical data?
Historical odds data is available on the Scale plan. We maintain closing lines and line movement history for backtesting your models.
